Supporting New Zealanders to acquire new capabilities throughput their working lives...led by the individual with active support from government.

We are developing a platform where individuals can register their skills and availability so that organisations can source specific skills as needed to complete an assignment, or for those wanting to pursue a career, providing a systematic approach to the development of skills aligned to opportunity.
